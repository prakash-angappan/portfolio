About Me

Engineering leadership, product development and interactive systems.

I am an engineering leader with experience building interactive gaming platforms and experiential installations used in live events and audience engagement environments. My work spans system architecture, game mechanics design, and leading cross-functional development teams to deliver immersive digital experiences. Over the years I have worked on projects involving gesture-based interaction systems, interactive kiosks, and experimental gameplay prototypes designed for public installations and marketing events. I enjoy exploring the intersection of technology, design, and human interaction — especially in areas where games, storytelling, and technology meet.

My professional interests include:

  • Game mechanics and interaction design
  • Real-time systems and gameplay architecture
  • Interactive installations and experiential technology
  • Building high-performance engineering teams
  • Exploring AI-assisted development tools
